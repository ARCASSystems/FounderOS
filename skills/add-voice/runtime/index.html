Founder OS - voice

Tier 0 - the default. No extra key, no paid service. Your one subscription answers; your browser hears and speaks.

Heads up: your browser's speech recognition may send your audio to your browser vendor (Google or Microsoft) to turn it into text. It needs no key and costs nothing, but it is not fully local. For fully-local speech, add the faster-whisper upgrade (see the add-voice skill's tiers note). Your brain - your files and answers - stays on this machine either way.
