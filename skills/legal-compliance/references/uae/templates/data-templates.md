### 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Personal Data"** means any data relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined in UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021.

**"Processing"** means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, recording, storage, modification, retrieval, use, disclosure, transfer, erasure, or destruction.

**"Data Subject"** means the individual to whom the Personal Data relates.

**"Sub-processor"** means a third party engaged by the Processor to process Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.

### 3. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS

The Processor shall:

3.1. Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Controller, unless required by law.

3.2. Ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data are subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

3.3. Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect Personal Data, including at minimum: [encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, regular security testing, incident response procedures].

3.4. Not engage any Sub-processor without prior written consent of the Controller. If consent is given, the Processor must impose equivalent data protection obligations on the Sub-processor and remain liable for the Sub-processor's performance.

3.5. Assist the Controller in responding to Data Subject requests (access, correction, deletion, portability) within [5] business days of notification.

3.6. Notify the Controller without undue delay (and in any event within [24/48/72] hours) upon becoming aware of a Personal Data breach.

3.7. Assist the Controller with data protection impact assessments and prior consultations with authorities, where required.

3.8. Upon termination of this DPA or the Main Agreement, at the Controller's choice: return all Personal Data to the Controller or delete it securely, and provide written certification of deletion. The Processor may retain copies only where required by law.

3.9. Make available to the Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA, and allow for and contribute to audits and inspections.
