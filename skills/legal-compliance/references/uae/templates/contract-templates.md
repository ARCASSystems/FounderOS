# Service Agreement Template

**Usage:** Adapt this template for consultancy, professional services, or project-based engagements between UAE entities. Replace bracketed fields with actual details. Have a qualified lawyer review the final version for significant engagements (AED 500K+).

**Note:** This is a starting point, not a final legal document. The drafter should adjust based on the specific relationship, risk profile, and commercial context.

---

## SERVICE AGREEMENT

**Date:** [Date]

**Between:**

**Service Provider:**
[Full Legal Name]

Represented by: [Name], [Title]
("Service Provider")

**Client:**
[Full Legal Name]
License No.: [Trade License Number]
Registered Address: [Full Address including Free Zone / Emirate]
Represented by: [Name], [Title]
("Client")

(each a "Party" and collectively the "Parties")

---

### 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1. The Service Provider shall provide the following services ("Services"):
[Describe services in detail — deliverables, milestones, acceptance criteria]

1.2. Any changes to the scope of Services shall be agreed in writing by both Parties through a Change Order signed by authorized representatives.

1.3. The Service Provider shall perform the Services with reasonable skill and care, in accordance with generally accepted professional standards.

---

### 2. TERM

2.1. This Agreement shall commence on [Start Date] and shall continue until [End Date / completion of Services / "until terminated in accordance with Section 8"].

2.2. [If applicable: This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of [duration] unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least [30/60/90] days before the end of the then-current term.]

---

### 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

3.1. In consideration of the Services, the Client shall pay the Service Provider:
[Choose one or combine:]
- A fixed fee of AED [amount] payable as follows: [payment schedule]
- A monthly retainer of AED [amount] payable on the [date] of each month
- Hourly rates as set out in Schedule A, invoiced [monthly/upon milestone completion]

3.2. All fees are exclusive of VAT. Where applicable, VAT shall be added at the prevailing rate and shown separately on invoices.

3.3. The Service Provider shall submit invoices to [email/address]. The Client shall pay each invoice within [14/30] days of receipt.

3.4. Late payments shall incur interest at [9/12]% per annum from the due date until payment in full.

3.5. [If applicable: The Client shall reimburse the Service Provider for pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses supported by receipts.]

---

### 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE CLIENT

4.1. The Client shall:
(a) Provide timely access to information, personnel, and resources reasonably required for the performance of the Services;
(b) Designate a representative authorized to make decisions and provide approvals;
(c) Review and provide feedback on deliverables within [X] business days of submission.

4.2. Delays caused by the Client's failure to meet its obligations under this Section shall not constitute a breach by the Service Provider and may result in adjusted timelines.

---

### 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1. **Work Product:** All work product, deliverables, and materials created by the Service Provider specifically for the Client under this Agreement ("Work Product") shall be the exclusive property of the Client upon full payment of the applicable fees.

5.2. **Pre-Existing IP:** Each Party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. The Service Provider grants the Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable license to use any pre-existing IP of the Service Provider that is incorporated into the Work Product, solely to the extent necessary for the Client to use the Work Product.

5.3. **Tools and Methodologies:** General tools, methodologies, frameworks, and know-how developed or used by the Service Provider (whether before or during this engagement) remain the property of the Service Provider.

---

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1. Each Party ("Receiving Party") shall maintain in confidence all information received from the other Party ("Disclosing Party") that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential ("Confidential Information").

6.2. Confidential Information excludes information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party before disclosure; (c) is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information; (d) is lawfully received from a third party without restriction.

6.3. The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to its employees and advisors on a need-to-know basis, provided they are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

6.4. Confidentiality obligations under this Section survive termination for a period of [2/3/5] years.

---

### 7. LIABILITY

7.1. The Service Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed [1x/2x the total fees paid or payable under this Agreement / the fees paid in the preceding 12 months].

7.2. Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or loss-of-profit damages, regardless of the form of action.

7.3. The limitations in this Section do not apply to: (a) breach of confidentiality obligations; (b) infringement of intellectual property rights; (c) fraud or willful misconduct; (d) payment obligations.

---

### 8. TERMINATION

8.1. **For Convenience:** Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing [30/60/90] days' written notice.

8.2. **For Cause:** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) commits a material breach that remains uncured for [14/30] days after written notice; (b) becomes insolvent or enters liquidation; (c) loses any license or regulatory approval required to perform its obligations.

8.3. **Effect of Termination:** Upon termination:
(a) The Client shall pay for all Services satisfactorily performed and expenses incurred up to the termination date;
(b) Each Party shall return or destroy the other's Confidential Information;
(c) The Service Provider shall deliver all completed and in-progress Work Product;
(d) Sections 5, 6, 7, and 10 shall survive termination.

---

### 9. FORCE MAJEURE

9.1. Neither Party shall be liable for failure or delay in performance caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, government actions, pandemics, war, or sanctions ("Force Majeure Event").

9.2. The affected Party shall notify the other Party within [7/14] days of becoming aware of the Force Majeure Event and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate its impact.

9.3. If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than [60/90] days, either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice without further liability.

---

### 10. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United Arab Emirates [/ the laws of the DIFC / the laws of the ADGM — choose based on entity types].

10.2. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved as follows:
(a) **Negotiation:** The Parties shall attempt to resolve the dispute through good faith negotiation within [30] days;
(b) **[Choose one: Courts / Arbitration]:**
- **Courts:** If negotiation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the competent courts of [Dubai / Abu Dhabi / DIFC / ADGM];
- **Arbitration:** If negotiation fails, the dispute shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration administered by [DIAC / DIFC-LCIA / ADCCAC] under its applicable rules. The seat of arbitration shall be [Dubai / Abu Dhabi]. The language of arbitration shall be English. The number of arbitrators shall be [one/three].

---

### 11. GENERAL

11.1. **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements relating to its subject matter.

11.2. **Amendments:** No amendment shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

11.3. **Assignment:** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's prior written consent, except to an affiliate or in connection with a merger or acquisition.

11.4. **Severability:** If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

11.5. **Notices:** All formal notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set out above [and/or by email to: [email addresses]].

11.6. **Language:** This Agreement is executed in English. [If bilingual: In the event of any conflict between the English and Arabic versions, the [English/Arabic] version shall prevail.]

# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Template

**Usage:** Adapt for any situation where confidential information is being shared between parties. This is a mutual NDA (both parties disclose). For a unilateral NDA (one party discloses), remove the mutual obligations and make it one-directional.

**Note:** Starting point, not a final legal document. Adjust for the specific context and relationship.

---

## MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**Date:** [Date]

**Between:**

**Party A:**
[Full Legal Name]
License No.: [Trade License Number]
Registered Address: [Full Address]
("Party A")

**Party B:**
[Full Legal Name]
License No.: [Trade License Number]
Registered Address: [Full Address]
("Party B")

(each a "Party" and collectively the "Parties")

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Parties intend to explore [describe the purpose — e.g., a potential business relationship, partnership, transaction, engagement] (the "Purpose"). In connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose Confidential Information to the other.

---

### 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

2.1. "Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other (the "Receiving Party"), whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, that:
(a) is designated as confidential, proprietary, or similarly marked; or
(b) a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

2.2. Confidential Information includes, without limitation: business plans, financial data, customer lists, pricing, technical information, product designs, software, trade secrets, methodologies, strategies, and any other proprietary information.

2.3. Confidential Information does **not** include information that:
(a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement;
(b) was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records;
(c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the Confidential Information;
(d) is lawfully received from a third party without restriction on disclosure.

---

### 3. OBLIGATIONS

3.1. The Receiving Party shall:
(a) Keep Confidential Information strictly confidential;
(b) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(c) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
(d) Limit access to employees and professional advisors who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement;
(e) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care.

3.2. The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information if required by law, regulation, or court order, provided it: (a) gives prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party (where legally permitted); (b) cooperates with any effort by the Disclosing Party to seek protective measures; and (c) discloses only the minimum information required.

---

### 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

4.1. No license or right is granted under this Agreement to any intellectual property, patent, trademark, copyright, or trade secret of either Party.

4.2. Disclosure of Confidential Information does not constitute or imply any transfer of ownership or license rights.

---

### 5. TERM AND RETURN OF INFORMATION

5.1. This Agreement shall remain in effect for [2/3/5] years from the date above, unless terminated earlier by either Party upon [30] days' written notice.

5.2. The confidentiality obligations under Section 3 shall survive termination or expiration for a period of [2/3/5] years. For trade secrets, obligations continue indefinitely for as long as the information qualifies as a trade secret.

5.3. Upon termination, expiration, or written request, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all copies of Confidential Information and, upon request, provide written certification of destruction.

---

### 6. REMEDIES

6.1. The Parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be an inadequate remedy. The Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief in addition to any other remedies available at law.

6.2. Nothing in this Agreement limits either Party's right to seek damages for breach.

---

### 7. NO WARRANTY

7.1. Confidential Information is provided "as is." Neither Party makes any representation or warranty regarding the accuracy, completeness, or fitness for purpose of any Confidential Information disclosed.

7.2. Neither Party is obligated to enter into any further agreement or transaction as a result of this Agreement.

---

### 8. GENERAL

8.1. **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates [/ DIFC / ADGM].

8.2. **Disputes:** Any dispute shall be resolved first through good faith negotiation. If unresolved within [30] days, disputes shall be submitted to [the competent courts of Dubai / DIFC Courts / ADGM Courts / arbitration at DIAC].

8.3. **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding regarding confidentiality between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements on this subject.

8.4. **Amendments:** No amendment shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

8.5. **Assignment:** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent of the other.

8.6. **Severability:** If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

8.7. **Language:** This Agreement is executed in English. [If bilingual: The [English/Arabic] version shall prevail in case of conflict.]
