# Employment Offer Letter Template

**Usage:** Adapt for new hire offers. This is a pre-contract document — the formal employment contract (registered with MOHRE) follows acceptance. Replace bracketed fields with actual details.

# Disciplinary Warning Letter Template

**Usage:** Issue when an employee has committed a workplace violation that requires formal documentation. This creates the paper trail needed for potential future termination under Article 44.

---

**[Company Letterhead / Company Name]**
**[Company Address]**

Date: [Date]
Reference: [WL-YYYY-###]

**CONFIDENTIAL**

To: [Employee Full Name]
Employee ID: [ID Number]
Department: [Department]
Job Title: [Job Title]

**Subject: [First / Second / Final] Written Warning — [Brief Description of Violation]**

---

Dear [Employee First Name],

This letter serves as a **[first / second / final] written warning** regarding your conduct/performance as described below.

### Incident Details

**Date of incident:** [Date]
**Description:** [Factual, objective description of what happened. Include: what the employee did or failed to do, when, where, and the impact on the business or colleagues. Stick to facts — avoid subjective language.]

**Relevant policy/contract clause:** [Reference the specific company policy, employment contract provision, or legal requirement that was violated.]

### Previous Actions

[If first warning: "You were verbally warned about this matter on [date]." / If second warning: "You received a first written warning on [date] regarding [same/similar issue]. Reference: [previous warning reference number]."]

### Expected Improvement

You are required to:
[Specific, measurable actions the employee must take — e.g., "attend all scheduled shifts on time", "complete assigned tasks by deadline", "refrain from using personal phone during working hours"]

**Improvement must be demonstrated by:** [Date — typically 30 days]

### Consequences

Failure to improve or any repetition of this conduct may result in further disciplinary action, up to and including termination of your employment in accordance with the UAE Labour Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 33/2021).

### Employee Acknowledgement

By signing below, you acknowledge receipt of this warning. Your signature does not indicate agreement with the contents — you have the right to submit a written response within [5/7] working days, which will be attached to this warning in your personnel file.

# Termination Notice Template

**Usage:** Formal notice of termination of employment. Adapt based on whether termination is with notice (standard) or without notice (Article 44 gross misconduct). Have HR or legal review before issuing for complex situations.

---

**[Company Letterhead / Company Name]**
**[Company Address]**

Date: [Date]
Reference: [TN-YYYY-###]

**CONFIDENTIAL**

To: [Employee Full Name]
Employee ID: [ID Number]
Department: [Department]
Job Title: [Job Title]
Date of Joining: [Start Date]

**Subject: Notice of Termination of Employment**

---

Dear [Employee First Name],

## [OPTION A — Standard Termination with Notice]

We regret to inform you that your employment with **[Company Name]** will be terminated effective **[Last Working Day]**, subject to a notice period of **[30/60/90] days** commencing from the date of this letter, in accordance with your employment contract.

**Reason for termination:** [Brief, factual statement of the reason — e.g., "business restructuring", "role redundancy", "performance-related following documented performance improvement process"]

---

## [OPTION B — Termination Without Notice (Article 44)]

We hereby terminate your employment with **[Company Name]** with immediate effect, effective **[today's date]**, without notice, pursuant to Article 44 of Federal Decree-Law No. 33/2021 (UAE Labour Law).

**Ground for immediate termination:** [Cite the specific Article 44 sub-clause and factual basis — e.g., "Article 44(2): committing an error resulting in substantial material loss to the employer, namely [describe incident]. This incident was reported to MOHRE on [date]."]

---

### During the Notice Period [Include only for Option A]

During the notice period, you are expected to:
- Continue performing your duties unless instructed otherwise
- Complete a handover of all ongoing work, files, and responsibilities to [designated person]
- Return all company property (laptop, phone, access cards, documents)

You are entitled to 1 paid day off per week during the notice period to seek alternative employment.

[Alternative: "You are released from the obligation to attend work during the notice period (garden leave). You will continue to receive your full salary and benefits during this time."]

### Final Settlement

Your final settlement will be calculated and paid within 14 days of your last working day, and will include:

| Component | Notes |
|---|---|
| Outstanding salary | Up to and including your last working day |
| Payment in lieu of unused annual leave | Based on basic salary |
| End-of-service gratuity | Calculated per UAE Labour Law |
| Repatriation air ticket | [One-way ticket to home country / cash equivalent] |
| [Any other entitlements] | [As per contract] |

**Deductions:** Any outstanding amounts owed to the company (loans, advances, damages) will be deducted from the final settlement.

### Visa Cancellation

Your employment visa will be cancelled following your last working day. You will have a **30-day grace period** after visa cancellation to either:
- Secure new employment and transfer your visa
- Exit the UAE
- Change your visa status

### Obligations After Termination

The following obligations survive the termination of your employment:
- Confidentiality obligations as per your employment contract and any NDA
- [If applicable: Non-compete obligations for a period of [duration] within [scope]]
- Return of all company property and confidential information

### Company Property

Please return the following by your last working day:
- [ ] Laptop / computer equipment
- [ ] Mobile phone (if company-provided)
- [ ] Access cards / keys
- [ ] Company documents and files
- [ ] [Other items]

---

We wish you well in your future endeavours.

Sincerely,

[Signatory Name]
[Signatory Title]
[Company Name]

---
