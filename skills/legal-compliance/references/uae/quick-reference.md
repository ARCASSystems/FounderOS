# UAE Quick Reference

**Last Verified: 2026-04-25**

Most-asked questions, answered directly. The skill checks here first before loading a full domain file.

---

## End-of-Service Gratuity

- **First 5 years:** 21 calendar days of BASIC salary per year of service
- **After 5 years:** 30 calendar days of BASIC salary per year of service
- **Cap:** 2 years' total remuneration
- **Daily rate:** Monthly basic salary ÷ 30
- Pro-rated for partial years
- Based on LAST basic salary only (excludes allowances)
- Paid within 14 days of last working day
- No gratuity if <1 year service or Article 44 dismissal

## VAT

- **Mandatory registration threshold:** AED 375,000 taxable supplies (trailing 12 months)
- **Voluntary registration threshold:** AED 187,500
- **Standard rate:** 5%
- **Filing:** Quarterly (default); monthly if revenue > AED 150M
- **Due:** 28th of the month following the tax period
- **Late filing penalty:** AED 1,000 first offense, AED 2,000 repeated within 24 months
- **Records retention:** 5 years (Arabic or English)

## Corporate Tax

- **Rate:** 0% on first AED 375,000 taxable income; 9% above
- **Free zone (QFZP):** 0% on qualifying income if all 6 conditions met (Cabinet Decision 37/2023 + MD 229/230/2025)
- **Small Business Relief:** Revenue ≤AED 3M = treat taxable income as zero. **NOT extended beyond 31 Dec 2026** (as of 2026-04-25)
- **Filing:** 9 months after financial year end
- **Late registration penalty:** AED 10,000

## Employment Contracts

- **All fixed-term post-Feb 2022.** No unlimited contracts. Maximum 3 years, renewable.
- **Notice periods:** Minimum 30 days, maximum 90 days (per contract). Probation: 14 days.
- **Probation:** Maximum 6 months, cannot be extended or repeated with same employer.
- **Working hours:** 8/day or 48/week. Ramadan reduced by 2 hours/day.

## Emiratisation (50+ employee firms, mainland)

| Year-end | Target rate (skilled jobs) |
|---|---|
| 31 Dec 2024 | 6% |
| 31 Dec 2025 | 8% |
| **31 Dec 2026** | **10%** |

- **Penalty:** AED 9,000/month (AED 108,000/year) per missing position
- **Emirati minimum wage (private sector):** AED 6,000/month effective 1 January 2026
- Free zone companies generally exempt; verify per zone

## MOHRE Binding Authority (post-2024 amendments)

- For employment disputes ≤AED 50,000, MOHRE issues binding decisions
- No court route required first
- General permit/dues fines: AED 100,000–AED 1,000,000 for hiring without valid permits or failing to settle dues

## Visa Grace Periods

- **Standard:** 30 days after cancellation
- **Skill Level 1/2 holders, Golden Visa, Green Visa, dependents:** up to 6 months
- **Visit visa:** 10-day grace removed; AED 50/day fines on overstay
- Grace clock starts on visa **expiry**, not cancellation date

## Personal Income Tax

- **No personal income tax.** No tax on salaries or personal investment income.

## E-Signatures (Federal Decree-Law 46/2021)

- **Valid for most commercial contracts.** DocuSign/PandaDoc legally recognised.
- **NOT valid for:** Personal status, real estate titles, negotiable instruments, POAs requiring notarisation.
