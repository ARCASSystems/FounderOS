# Current Limits

Founder OS is early. It is useful when it has real context and dangerous when it pretends to know what it has not seen.

## What it will not do yet

- It will not run your business while you are asleep.
- It will not replace your judgment on clients, pricing, hiring, or relationships.
- It will not know what happened in WhatsApp, email, calls, or meetings unless you capture it.
- It will not silently write to your live files without showing you what changed.
- It will not make a weak offer strong by wrapping it in better language.

## What it should do well

- Keep priorities, decisions, clients, and cadence in one place.
- Catch repeated stalls before they become invisible.
- Turn messy voice notes into structured next actions.
- Force source-backed recommendations instead of generic AI advice.
- Help you close the gap between what happened and what the system knows.

## Proof standard

Before adding more product surface, Founder OS must prove one complete loop:

1. Show today's operating state.
2. Help send or prepare a real revenue-facing action.
3. Capture the response or lack of response.
4. Log the next decision.

If that loop does not work, more features are decoration.
