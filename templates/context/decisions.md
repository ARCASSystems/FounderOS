# Decisions

> All open, parked, and recently resolved decisions. Updated as decisions move through states.
> Check this file at session start during the Chief of Staff scan.

---

## Pending

Decisions that are open and need to be made. Actively blocking or soon will be.

**Format** - each pending decision is its own `###` heading (newest at the top), followed by these fields:

- **Since:** [DATE]
- **Decision:** [what needs to be decided]
- **Blocking:** [what this is holding up]
- **Options:** [brief list]
- **Leaning toward:** [if any]

## Parked

Decisions that can't be made yet. Trigger condition not met. Also tracked in `brain/decisions-parked.md`.

**Format** - each parked decision is its own `###` heading, followed by these fields:

- **Parked:** [DATE]
- **Trigger to revisit:** [specific condition]
- **Context:** [why it's parked]

## Resolved

Recently closed decisions. Keep last 90 days. Archive older ones.

**Format** - each resolved decision is its own `###` heading, followed by these fields:

- **Decided:** [DATE]
- **Decision made:** [what was decided]
- **Rationale:** [why]
