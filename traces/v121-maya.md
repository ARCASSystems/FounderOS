*Captured as red_flags.*

**Q12 (Anti-example pairs):** "Three BAD/GOOD pairs from your own writing patterns."

**Maya:**
"Bad: 'Stillpoint will help you find balance in a hectic world.'
Good: 'Two minutes. Nobody asking you anything.'
Rule: Cut benefit claims. Show the moment instead.

Bad: 'Our science-backed approach to mindfulness...'
Good: 'The two-minute pause that actually works because it fits into the gap between one thing ending and another starting.'
Rule: No credential-signaling. Show the mechanism.

Bad: 'Join thousands of women who are transforming their daily lives.'
Good: 'She opened it in the parking lot before picking up her kids.'
Rule: No scale bragging. One specific person."
